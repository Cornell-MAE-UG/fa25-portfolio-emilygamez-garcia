I found the first homework assignment to be the most valuable because it served as an introduction to all three modes of heat transfer. The assignment’s first question is conceptual and teaches me to meticulously think through the different interactions of heat transfer in familiar cases below the atmosphere and in unprecedented environments such as outer space. The final two questions teach me how to quantify the modes of heat transfer for both small and large scale everyday examples. The last question is especially helpful because it does not neglect radiation, a trend I’ve noticed in other problems. It teaches me that I can account for the presence of radiation without overcomplicating my system. I know this assignment will be helpful when solving more advanced problems in the course and in my future profession. I can reference my work to build a foundational understanding before adding the intricacies and requirements of what I am specifically focusing on.
